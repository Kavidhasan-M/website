Story behind Teamlyst

During 2nd year of my college, I wanted to build a website. I had some knowledge in frontend development and no idea about the backend. I started looking for a person who can help me in developing backend for the website.

Luckily, I found a guy who can help me in developing backend for the website and he was from my class. One day, I was discussing with him the problem I had in finding someone to work with me. That's when the question in us raised "Why don't we work on this?". Finally, we teamed up again for building Teamlyst.

Between this

I was responsible for conducting user reseach, designing interfaces, and frontend development.

The discussion showed that we were facing the same issue. I wanted to know if other students face the same problem. This led me to conduct a research. I started conducting quantitative analysis using Google Forms.

I almost went to all the dormotories in my hostel to conduct qualitative analysis. I had casual conservation with the students and this helped me to understand their problem.

Insights

  1. Many students gave up their ideas without working on them if there was no support.
  2. Many students who possessed some level of expertise in a particular area, but lacked an opportunity to showcase their abilities.
  3. There is no direct way to find teammates to work on projects.

Features

Depending on the tasks that users will carry out on this platform, the following tasks are implemented as features,

  • Explore the projects available to join in the team.
  • Search people based on skills.
  • Post about the project they are working on or about to work with roles available to join the team.
  • Status about their request to the team builder.
  • Check who wants to work in the team

Design

After deciding the features, I made some sketches of the interface. Then, I started designing the interfaces in Figma.






Iterations

Before we had CTA buttons for signup and login on the landing page. After finding that the users hesitates to create an account, we allowed users to explore the projects available in the platform.

The users need to signup if they are interested to join in a team.

Outcomes

We onboarded 90+ users into the platform and 4 teams were formed using Teamlyst.
